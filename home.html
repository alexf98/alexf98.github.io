About Us

Speaking about Roy's Basement, it is a sports team started by Mr. Ariel Roy Francisco, who has been based in the US since the 1980's. Why the name Roy's Basement? Kabayan Ariel Roy has an awesome pool table in the basement of his lovely home in Gainesville, Virginia, where his stakehorses practice and play. In my opinion, it is one cool team name. What few actually know is that kabayan Ariel Roy sponsored notable Filipino pool players way before the Roy's Basement name was coined. These include, but are not limited to, the great Jose "Amang" Parica, Francisco "Django" Bustamante, Leonardo "Dodong Dadiangas" Andam. Team Roy's Basement actively sponsors and take under its wings, pool players - mostly Filipinos - whenever they are in the US. These include World Champions Dennis Orcollo and Carlo Biado, 2x-All Japan Open Champion Johann Chua, Andy Mercer Open Champion Warren Kiamco, champ Jeffrey Ignacio, etc. And oh! Roy's Basement also manages Ruslan Chinahov - the Siberian Express. The team travels the US extensively, entering major tournaments there. Truth be told, its horses are a force to reckon with!
